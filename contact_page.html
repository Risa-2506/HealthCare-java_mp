Frequently Asked Questions

Quick question And Answers

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, Medicare, and Medicaid.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment online through our patient portal, by calling our appointment line at (555) 123-4569, or by visiting us in person.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, valid ID, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we have a 24/7 emergency department staffed with board-certified emergency physicians and equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal online, or request copies by contacting our medical records department.

What are your visiting hours?

General visiting hours are 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. ICU and special units may have different hours. Please check with the specific department.
